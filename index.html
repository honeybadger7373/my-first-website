SEA FISH

Fish are one of the animals belonging to the vertebrate family. In addition, tetrapods are excluded from vertebrates. In general, it lives in the water, breathes with gills, moves with fins, and is affected by ambient temperature as a cold-blooded animal whose body surface is covered with scales.

In addition, fish are distributed in various aquatic environments on Earth, and their habitats vary from tropical to polar regions, inland freshwater regions to seawater regions, and also from the surface of the ocean to the deep sea. The total number of fish species is about 25,000 to 31, 000, which is the majority of all vertebrates.
